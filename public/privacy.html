Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 30, 2024

1. Introduction

Neural Crypto Scanner ("we," "our," or "the Service") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Telegram User Data: Username, user ID, and profile photo (if you use Telegram Web App)
  • TON Wallet Address: Your public wallet address when you connect via TON Connect
  • Transaction Data: Records of boost package purchases

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Session Data: Simulated wallet scanning results, balance information
  • Usage Statistics: Number of scans performed, boost package selections
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device information, IP address

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Service functionality
  • Process boost package purchases via TON Connect
  • Display your Telegram username and avatar in the interface
  • Store simulation progress and settings
  • Improve and optimize the Service
  • Communicate important updates about the Service

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Session Storage

Your simulation data (balances, scanned wallets) is stored in temporary session storage and is cleared when you close the application or after 7 days of inactivity.

4.2 Security Measures

  • We NEVER store or have access to your Secret Recovery Phrase

5. TON Connect and Wallet Integration

When you connect your TON wallet:

  • Only your public wallet address is shared with the Service
  • We can only request transactions; you must explicitly confirm each one
  • We cannot access your wallet funds without your confirmation
  • You can disconnect your wallet at any time through TON Wallet settings

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 CoinGecko API

We use CoinGecko API to fetch current cryptocurrency prices for USD conversion. This service may collect anonymous usage statistics.

6.2 TON Connect

TON Connect is provided by the TON Foundation. Please review their privacy policy at ton.org

6.3 Telegram Web App

If you access the Service through Telegram, Telegram's privacy policy applies to data collected by their platform.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Session Data: 7 days or until session expires
  • Transaction Records: 90 days for accounting purposes
  • Usage Statistics: Anonymized and aggregated indefinitely

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your stored data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Opt-Out: Disconnect your wallet and stop using the Service

9. Cookies and Local Storage

We use session cookies and browser local storage to:

  • Maintain your session state
  • Remember your preferences
  • Store simulation progress

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.
